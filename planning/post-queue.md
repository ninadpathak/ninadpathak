## Writing rules (enforce on every post)

- No em dashes (—)
- No semicolons in prose
- No double dashes (--)
- No sentences starting with: In, This, By, Finally, Most, Ever, If
- No contrastive parallels ("not X, but Y")
- No buildup/announcement sentences
- First-person voice throughout ("I", "my", "I've seen")
- No forbidden words: leverage, synergy, unlock, gap, distance
- 1500–2500 words
- Add internal links to 2–3 related published posts
