# Repurposing One Technical Post Into Ten Assets

You spent a week writing a comprehensive tutorial. It published. It performed well. Now it sits in your blog archive.

That post contains ten pieces of content. Extract them.

## 1. The Twitter Thread

Pull out five key insights. Write each as a standalone tweet. Connect them with a narrative thread.

Include code snippets as images. Screenshots of your terminal work well. Visuals stop the scroll.

## 2. The LinkedIn Post

Rewrite the introduction for a professional audience. Focus on business outcomes. Focus on career impact.

LinkedIn audiences care about skill development. Frame your content as professional growth.

## 3. The Video Tutorial

Record your screen following your own tutorial. Narrate each step. Show the errors that happen. Show how to fix them.

Post to YouTube. Post to TikTok. Post to Instagram Reels. Different platforms reach different audiences.

## 4. The Newsletter Issue

Summarize the post in 300 words. Include the key code snippet. Link to the full tutorial for details.

Your newsletter audience wants awareness. They will click if the summary sparks interest.

## 5. The Conference Talk

Expand the post into a 20 minute presentation. Add context about why the problem matters. Add war stories from implementation.

Submit to relevant conferences. Local meetups work well for testing material.

## 6. The Documentation Page

Technical posts often belong in docs. Adapt the tutorial into your official documentation.

Update the tone. Documentation is more formal than blog posts. The information stays valuable.

## 7. The Cheat Sheet

Extract commands. Extract configuration examples. Extract key concepts. Format as a one page PDF.

Developers love cheat sheets. They print them. They bookmark them. They share them.

## 8. The FAQ Entry

What questions did readers ask? Turn each question into an FAQ entry. Link back to the relevant section of the original post.

This builds your knowledge base. This improves self service support.

## 9. The Podcast Episode

Discuss the topic on a podcast. Explain the problem verbally. Describe the solution conversationally.

Audio reaches people during commutes. During workouts. During chores.

## 10. The Course Module

Combine multiple related posts into a course. Add exercises. Add quizzes. Add a certificate.

Courses command higher perceived value. They generate leads. They generate revenue.

---

One post is never just one post. It is source material. Extract every drop of value. Distribute across every channel your audience uses.
