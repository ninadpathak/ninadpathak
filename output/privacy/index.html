Privacy Policy

This site keeps data collection minimal. This page covers what I collect, what third-party tools do, and how to get in touch about it.

What I collect

If you email me, book a call, or contact me through a third-party service linked on this site, I receive the information you choose to share. That usually includes your name, email address, company name, and project details.

I do not sell personal data.

Analytics and third-party services

This site may use standard analytics, embedded scheduling tools, and social platforms that collect their own usage data through cookies or similar technologies.

Third-party services linked or embedded on this site may include:

  • Google
  • Zcal
  • LinkedIn
  • X

Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

Cookies

This site may use basic cookies or local storage for functionality such as theme preference, analytics, and embedded tools.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some parts of the site may work differently.

How I use information

I use submitted information to:

  • respond to inquiries
  • schedule calls
  • discuss projects
  • improve the site and content

Data retention

I keep messages and inquiry details for as long as they are useful for communication, recordkeeping, or ongoing business discussions.

Your rights

If you want me to delete an email thread or any personal information you shared directly with me, email me at hello@ninadpathak.com.

Contact

For privacy questions, email hello@ninadpathak.com.
