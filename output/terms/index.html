What this site is

ninadpathak.com publishes technical writing — articles, tutorials, implementation guides, and case studies. It is a portfolio and editorial site, not a SaaS product or service platform.

Using the content

The articles and case studies here represent original work. The copyright belongs to me unless a piece was commissioned by and explicitly credited to another publication.

What you can do without asking:

  • Read anything on the site
  • Link to any article or page
  • Quote short passages (a paragraph or two) with attribution and a link back to the source
  • Reference articles in your own writing, citing them as you would any other published work

What requires permission:

  • Republishing full articles elsewhere, including on your own site, newsletter, or publication
  • Translating or adapting articles for redistribution

To request permission, email hello@ninadpathak.com. I'm generally open to it with proper attribution.

Accuracy and limitations

I write technical content and I test what I can. That said:

  • Technical landscapes change fast. An article accurate at publication may become outdated as tools, APIs, and frameworks evolve.
  • I do not guarantee that every code snippet will work in every version of every dependency.
  • External links point to third-party sites I don't control. Their content and availability can change.

If you find an error — factual, technical, or otherwise — I want to know. Email the correction to hello@ninadpathak.com.
