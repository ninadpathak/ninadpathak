Why Technical Writing Matters in SaaS

Exploring the impact of clear documentation on SaaS growth.

Why documentation is your best growth engine#

Most startups treat documentation as an afterthought. But great docs can be your best marketing tool.

The problem with most docs#

  • Written for engineers, not users
  • Full of jargon and assumptions
  • Rarely updated

What good documentation looks like#

  • Explains the why, not just the how
  • Uses real-world examples
  • Gets updated as the product evolves

How to get there#

  • Talk to your users
  • Test your docs with real people
  • Make documentation a core part of your product process
