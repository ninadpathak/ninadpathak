My Content Process

How I create technical content that actually works for your business.

Creating technical content that drives results requires understanding, strategy, and collaboration. Here’s how I approach every project:

1. Deep Discovery#

I start by asking the tough questions—about your product, your users, and your goals. I’ll dig into your docs, talk to your team, and even test your product myself. This helps me uncover what makes your tech unique and what your audience actually needs.

2. Strategic Planning#

Once I understand your product, I map out a content plan. This includes:

  • Identifying key topics and user pain points
  • Outlining the types of content that will be most effective (guides, tutorials, API docs, etc.)
  • Creating a roadmap so you know what’s coming and why

3. Collaborative Drafting#

I don’t disappear and return with a finished product. Instead, I share outlines and drafts early, so you can give feedback and we can refine the direction together. This ensures the content is accurate, on-brand, and genuinely useful.

4. Clear, Human Writing#

I translate complex technical concepts into clear, engaging content that real people can understand. No jargon overload, no fluff—just value.

5. Review & Iterate#

You get the chance to review everything. I’m quick to respond to feedback and make revisions, so the final product is exactly what you need.

6. Delivery & Support#

Once approved, I deliver the content in your preferred format (Markdown, Google Docs, CMS-ready, etc.). I’m available for follow-up questions or tweaks as your needs evolve.
